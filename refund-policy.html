Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

We want you to have an amazing experience at The Light Park. This Refund Policy explains when and how you can request a refund for your purchase.

Ticket Refunds

Timeframe Refund
More than 24 hours before event Full refund
Within 24 hours of event Date change only (subject to availability)
No-show / After event start time No refund
Date Changes: We understand plans change. You may change your ticket date one time at no additional charge if done at least 24 hours before your original ticket date, subject to availability.

Weather-Related Closures

If The Light Park closes due to severe weather or safety concerns:

  • Full closure before your entry: Full refund or complimentary date change
  • Closure during your visit: Evaluated case-by-case; may receive partial refund or return pass
  • Light rain or cold weather (park remains open): No refund; we operate rain or shine in most conditions

Add-Ons and Upgrades

Item Refund Policy
VIP Upgrades Refundable if ticket is refunded; follows ticket policy
Photo Packages (pre-purchased) Refundable up to 24 hours before event
Photo Packages (purchased on-site) No refund after delivery
Food & Beverages No refund (unless defective)

Merchandise

  • Defective items: Full refund or exchange within 30 days with receipt
  • Change of mind: Exchange only within 14 days with receipt; item must be unused with tags attached
  • Clearance/Sale items: Final sale, no refunds or exchanges

In-Park Purchases

Please Note: All in-park purchases of food, beverages, and services (such as photos taken by our staff) are final. Please confirm your order before completing payment.

How to Request a Refund

  1. Email: Send your request to info@thelightpark.com with your order number and reason for refund
  2. Include: Order confirmation, ticket date, and contact information
  3. Response time: We will respond within 2-3 business days

Refund Processing

  • Approved refunds will be credited to the original payment method
  • Processing time: 5-10 business days depending on your bank
  • You will receive an email confirmation when your refund is processed

Exceptions

We reserve the right to make exceptions to this policy on a case-by-case basis at our sole discretion. Extenuating circumstances (medical emergencies, etc.) will be considered with appropriate documentation.
